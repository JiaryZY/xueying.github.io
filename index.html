    • PROJECT: THE 7 CONTINENTS MARATHON

      "Set a goal so big you can't achieve it until you grow into the person who can." - Unknown

      North America
      USA
      2012 Finisher
      05:45:26

      My first marathon and first endurance race that lasted 5:45:26. Reaching 30,898 entries, the 40th Honolulu Marathon was the second largest marathon in America in 2012. The exotic location in Hawaii and the aloha spirit made the race a popular destination among first-time marathoners.
      Europe
      Greece
      2013 Finisher
      04:55:58

      The classic route from Marathon to Athens. This race commemorates the run of the soldier Pheidippides from the town of Marathon to Athens in 490 B.C., bringing news of a Greek victory over the Persians. This IAAF Gold Label Road Race is perhaps one of the most difficult major marathon.
      Asia
      Japan
      2014 Finisher
      04:25:28

      An IAAF Gold Label marathon. One of the six World Marathon Majors, and the only one located in Asia. In 2014, the event drew over 300,000 applicants and less than 10% received a bib number to run the scenic course from Tokyo Metropolitan Office building to the Tokyo Big Sight.
      Asia
      North Korea
      2014 Finisher
      04:06:27

      I was the first amateur runner from Taiwan to ever participated in this full marathon race. Achieving a person best of 4:06:27, I was also the last runner before the official closed the gate. This mysterious and highly competitive event took place at Pyongyang, the capital of North Korea.
      Africa
      Algeria
      2015 Finisher
      06:35:39

      We were the first group of Taiwanese ever participated in this race. This race in the Sahara dessert is a mix of great challenge and extraodinary scenery. Runners will also pass through 3 Saharawi refugee camps in this course.
      Oceania
      Australia
      2015 Finisher
      05:53:15

      The race is held in the Australian outback, near the iconic Ayers Rock, which is the largest monolith in the world. You run on its famous ‘red earth’, on sand trails, bush tracks and dirt tracks.
      South America
      Chile
      2015 Finisher
      05:29:45

      Surrounded by blue lakes, snow peaks, and white glacier, this is of the most beautiful trail marathons. The race is held at the famous Torres del Paine National Park.
      Antarctica
      South Pole
      2015 Finisher
      07:29:35

      Southernmost marathon on earth, the only official marathon on mainland Antarctica and the only one within the Antarctic circle.
